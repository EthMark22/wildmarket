Our Evolution

2022
Foundation Through Experimentation

We started as a grassroots, research-first initiative built on collaboration, risk-taking, and curiosity. Operating as a DAO, we brought together traders, analysts, developers, and creatives to navigate the volatile but opportunity-rich world of Web3.

- Formed an active Russian-speaking DAO community

- Built and managed high-risk crypto and NFT portfolios

- Collaborated with established DAOs and ecosystem players

- Delivered deep-dive research in macroeconomics, crypto trends, and NFT markets

- Provided consulting services and launched early experimental trading strategies

2023
Growth & Infrastructure

With growing expertise and community support, we expanded into multi-asset portfolio management, validator operations, and retroactive airdrop strategies ("retro-hunting").

- Structured diversified portfolios for early-stage investors

- Developed educational content on trading, NFTs, and testnet farming

- Launched a large-scale airdrop farming program with 100+ wallets

- Ran validator nodes, optimized reward flows, and refined portfolio risk management

This year laid the foundation for systematic experimentation and cross-market strategy design.

2024
Vision Becomes Product

As markets matured, so did we. We capitalized on the bear market by rotating capital into undervalued assets and began transitioning from manual to automated trading systems.

- Locked in profits, rebalanced our portfolios across tokens and NFTs

- Explored meme coin narratives through short-term trading models

- Began building internal tools for data analysis and real-time decision-making

- Rebranded to reflect our new mission: to become an autonomous algorithmic trading fund

- Expanded our team with quants, market makers, and data engineers

This marked a key turning point: from experimentation to focused execution.

2025
Execution at Scale

Today, we are constructing the technical and strategic backbone of our vision:

- Building a proprietary algorithmic market-making fund

- Designing automated trading strategies: mean reversion, momentum capture, and "knife catching"

- Scaling internal research into production-grade trading models

- Launching infrastructure to support low-latency execution and deep market integration

- Backed by private investors, we're entering the next stage with conviction and clarity

2026
Algorithmic Fund Launch

Full deployment of our autonomous algorithmic trading fund with cross-market capabilities. Integration of machine learning models for predictive signals and expansion of asset coverage to emergent markets.

2027
Market Making & Capital Expansion

Scaling our market making operations across multiple venues and asset classes. Building infrastructure for external capital allocation with institutional-grade risk management and compliance systems.
